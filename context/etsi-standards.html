Other ways to get ETSI standards

  • The National Standards Organizations (NSOs) assist us in the making of European Standards (ENs). You can buy the standards from these NSOs.
  • The National Standardization Bodies (NSBs) assist us in the making of European Standards (ENs) and European standardization deliverables in response to Standardization Requests to support the European legislation or policies, in accordance with the amended Regulation (EU) No 1025/2012.
  • There are international distributors around the world from which you can also obtain ETSI standards.
